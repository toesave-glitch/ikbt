How It Works

Brain Trainer is a stream-of-consciousness writing engine. You choose a mode, and the clock automatically starts counting your active writing time.

Free Thought

Write whatever is on your mind. No rules, no direction. Pure freewriting.

Topic-Specific

Choose a subject and write everything you know about it. Great for studying.

Positive Mindset

Respond to a guided prompt. Reflective, intentional, growth-focused.

The Dual Clock

You have two clocks running:

Active Time: Counts only while you're typing. This becomes your XP (1 minute = 1 XP).

Pause Budget: If you stop typing for more than 3 seconds, the pause clock starts. You have 30 seconds of pause time total. When it runs out, your session ends automatically.

The goal: stay in flow. Write continuously. Stop for too long, and time's up.
